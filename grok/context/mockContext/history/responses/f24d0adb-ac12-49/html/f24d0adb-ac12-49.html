Big O Notation

O(n^2)

Turing Machine Transition Function

The transition function of a Turing machine can be represented as:

\delta(q, a) = (q', a', d)

where qqq and q′q'q′ are states, aaa and a′a'a′ are symbols, and ddd is the direction of movement.

Halting Problem

The halting problem, which is undecidable, can be expressed as:

\text{Halt}(P, I)

Given a program PPP and input III, it is impossible to determine in general whether PPP will halt on III.

Regular Expression

A regular expression for a language that accepts strings of aaas and bbbs where the number of aaas is even can be written as:

(a^*b^*)^*
